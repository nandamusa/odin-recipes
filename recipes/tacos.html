Tacos Recipes

Description

No boring tacos here! These chicken bacon ranch tacos are made with seasoned and grilled chicken, lettuce, red onions, tomatoes, pepper Jack cheese, ranch, and bacon. These tacos are uniqueâ€”you might never eat a taco without ranch again.

Ingredient

  • 6 slices bacon
  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 6 (6 inch) flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.

  2. Step 2

    Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels.

  3. Step 3

    Place tortillas on the hot grill and heat until lightly grilled, 20 to 30 seconds per side.
